About Us

Welcome to Memo, your trusted car rental service located at Alija Komerc, MK, 11th of November, Kumanovo 1300. We offer a range of quality, budget-friendly vehicles to suit every need. At Memo, we understand the importance of flexibility and affordability in car rentals. Our fleet includes compact hatchbacks like the Hyundai I20 and Citroen C3, spacious SUVs like the Hyundai Tucson, and stylish sedans such as the Dacia Logan and Citroen Elysse. We are committed to providing reliable and well-maintained cars at low prices, ensuring a hassle-free experience every time you choose Memo. Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or need a temporary replacement vehicle, Memo has got you covered. Experience the convenience and affordability of renting with Memo. We look forward to serving you soon!
